NORTH INDIA

North India is an incredible tourist destination for mountain lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The entire north India region comprises of scenic mountains, picturesque lakes, deep valleys, ancient temples, formidable forts and historical monuments. With all these characteristics tourist places in North India promise for a great holiday vacation. All the North Indian States draw lots of tourists from all across the World due to its rich cultural heritage and glorious past. So check the list of all famous tourist places to visit in North India that will never disappoint you.

  1. Kullu & Manali, Himachal Pradesh
  2. Rishikesh, Uttarakhand
  3. Ladakh, Gulmarg & Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir
  4. Mussoorie, Uttarakhand
  5. Kedarnath, Uttarakhand
  6. Amritsar, Punjab
  7. Agra, Uttar Pradesh
  8. Jaipur, Udaipur & Jaisalmer, Rajasthan
  9. Delhi
  10. Khajjiar, Himachal Pradesh
  11. Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh
